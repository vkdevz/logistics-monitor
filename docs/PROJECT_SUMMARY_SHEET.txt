  Opportunity:         $2,430,000 annual savings (7.7% of total loss)

    ✓ Seasonal rerouting (monsoon bypass routes): Avoids weather
    ✓ Consolidation hubs (Malaysia, Singapore): Batching benefits

  Investment:         $50K (software development)
  Expected Delay Reduction: 20% improvement on top 15 routes

    ✓ Weather Rerouting Intelligence: $1.35M savings

    ✓ Carrier Capacity Reservation: $1.70M savings
      → Emergency expediting slots (5% of FedEx/DHL capacity)
    
    ✓ Customs Expediting Program: $2.25M savings
      → Priority processing ($250/shipment), save 4-5 hours customs hold
    
    ✓ Real-Time Visibility & IoT Tracking: $1.20M savings
      → Reduce claim disputes, enable early intervention
  
  Timeline:            3 months to activate all 5 protocols
  Investment:         $817K (APIs, tracking, broker contracts)
  Expected Delay Prevention: 25% of all delays prevented
